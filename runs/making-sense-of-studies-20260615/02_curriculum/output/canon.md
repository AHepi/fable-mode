## 1. Naming registry (course-local)
Shared terms from series-canon. Course-local terms (this course owns them):
- **null hypothesis** = the starting assumption that there is *no real effect / no difference*.
- **p-value** = the probability of data at least this extreme *if the null were true*. Always stated
  with the "if the null were true" clause — never shortened to "probability it's chance".
- **statistically significant** = result unlikely under the null (a threshold call), explicitly NOT
  "big/important".
- **confidence interval** = a range estimate with a long-run-coverage meaning (M3).
Keep one name each; gloss every term in plain words on first use.
