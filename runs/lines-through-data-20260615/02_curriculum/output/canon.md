Internal consistency contract. **Sits under** the series-canon (`runs/statistics-20260615/series-canon.md`)

callbacks to Essentials. On a shared concept, the series-canon wins.

## 1. Naming registry (course-local)
Shared terms from series-canon. Course-local terms (this course owns them):
- **scatter plot** = each subject plotted as a point (x, y); the **cloud** = the set of points.
- **correlation** = strength + direction of a *straight-line* relationship, on a −1 to +1 scale.
  Always qualify "linear" when stating what correlation 0 means.
- **best-fit line** = the single straight line closest to all points (smallest vertical misses
  overall); a *summary of the average trend*. Never "regression equation"/formula; never derived.
- **lurking variable** (= confounding variable) = a hidden third thing that drives both measurements.
  Pick one name — **lurking variable** — and gloss "(also called a confounder)" once.
