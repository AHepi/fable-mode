A worked example of reading a political platform like an analyst rather than a partisan. Across four
short courses you build the shared diagnosis and vocabulary first, then take each policy area apart —
always attributing claims to the party and always pairing a policy with its critics. By the end you
can hold your own in the argument, on any side.
