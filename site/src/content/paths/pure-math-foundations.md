Two courses that together open the door to modern pure mathematics. Abstract algebra teaches you to
reason about *structure* — what stays true when you only know the rules of an operation. Topology
teaches you to reason about *space* — nearness and continuity stripped of distance. Take them in
either order; together they are the vocabulary the rest of advanced maths is written in.
