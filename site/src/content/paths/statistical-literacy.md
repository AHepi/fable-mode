Most of us meet statistics as a wall of jargon and take the claims on trust. This path takes you from
zero to genuinely **statistically literate** — able to look at a headline number, a poll, or a graph
and ask the right question. It begins with the Essentials primer that gives you the vocabulary, then
applies it to the two situations you meet most: judging a study, and reading a relationship between
two things.
