The Opportunity Party's 2026 Platform

Understand a whole political platform well enough to argue any side of it — neutrally, from the mechanics up.

A worked example of reading a political platform like an analyst rather than a partisan. Across four short courses you build the shared diagnosis and vocabulary first, then take each policy area apart — always attributing claims to the party and always pairing a policy with its critics. By the end you can hold your own in the argument, on any side.

By the end you can
  • Explain the mechanism of each major policy in plain words.
  • State the problem each policy targets and how it fits New Zealand.
  • Give the strongest critique of each — enough to debate, not parrot.
